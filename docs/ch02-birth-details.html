Birth Details & Panchangaजन्म तपशील व पंचांग

The first thing an astrologer asks is: when, and where. Three small numbers — date, time, place — and from them the entire sky at the moment of your first breath can be reconstructed. This chapter records those numbers for Pushkar Prakash Jawale, then introduces Panchanga, the five-limbed Vedic calendar that turns a clock-time into a living moment. Beyond the bare five limbs, it also unpacks the Avakahada Chakra — the thirteen-cell signature that classical Marathi panchangas use to compress an entire birth-moment into a single readable grid.

कोणताही ज्योतिषी सर्वप्रथम तीनच प्रश्न विचारतो: केव्हा? कुठे? आणि नेमका वेळ कोणता? — कारण याच तीन नोंदींच्या आधारावर जन्माच्या क्षणाचे संपूर्ण आकाश गणितशास्त्राच्या मदतीने पुन्हा रचता येते. तारीख मंद-गती ग्रह कुठे होते ते सांगते (शनी एका राशीत अडीच वर्षे राहतो, तर सूर्य एका राशीत महिनाभर), वेळ सर्वात महत्त्वाची असते कारण ती लग्न (पूर्वेला उगवणारी राशी) ठरवते — आणि लग्न तर दर ४ मिनिटांत १ अंशाने बदलते. म्हणूनच जन्माची वेळ कितीही जुनी असली तरी ती अचूक नोंदलेली असणे आवश्यक. स्थळसुद्धा महत्त्वाचे — कल्याण-अंबरनाथ परिसर आणि नागपूर या दोन ठिकाणी एकाच मिनिटी जन्मलेल्या दोन बाळांची लग्ने थोडी वेगळी असतात कारण आकाश हे स्थानिक असते. हा अध्याय पुष्करच्या जन्माच्या या तीन नोंदी अचूक मांडतो आणि नंतर पंचांग — वैदिक कालगणनेची पाच अंगे (तिथी, वार, नक्षत्र, योग, करण) — समजावून सांगतो. पंचांग म्हणजे फक्त "हिंदू कॅलेंडर" नाही; तो जन्माच्या क्षणाचा "गुणवत्तेचा अहवाल" आहे — कोणत्या प्रकारची हवा होती, कोणती ऊर्जा वाहत होती, कोणत्या ग्रहांचे संगीत वाजत होते — हे सांगणारा. शेवटी अवकहडा चक्र — महाराष्ट्रीय पंचांगांत वापरली जाणारी १३ कोष्टकांची एकत्रित ओळख-तालिका — सुद्धा या अध्यायात उलगडली आहे.

Why each number mattersप्रत्येक नोंदीचे महत्त्व

Date determines the position of the slow planets — Sun, Saturn, Jupiter, Rahu, Ketu — within a day or two of accuracy. Time is more critical: it determines the Lagna (rising sign), which moves through all twelve signs every 24 hours. A four-minute error in birth time can shift the lagna by one degree; an error of two hours can change the lagna entirely. Place matters because the same moment in Tokyo and in Mumbai produces different rising signs — the sky a person sees overhead is local. For Pushkar, born late evening at 21:43 IST in the Kalyan-Ambernath corridor, the slow grahas had been broadly fixed for weeks (Saturn deep into its Gemini retrograde, Jupiter early in Leo, Rahu-Ketu on the Aries-Libra axis), while the fast-moving Lagna had just rotated Cancer up to nearly 22° on the eastern horizon — moments away from Leo rising.

तारीख — १७ डिसेंबर २००३ — यावरून मंद ग्रहांची स्थिती एक-दोन दिवसांच्या अचूकतेने ठरते. म्हणजे शनी मिथुनात वक्री, गुरू सिंहाच्या सुरुवातीला, राहू-केतू मेष-तूळ अक्षावर, सूर्य धनूत १° वर — हे सर्व तारखेवरून निश्चित होते. वेळ — रात्री ९:४३ — ही सर्वात नाजूक नोंद आहे, कारण यावरून लग्न ठरते. लग्न दर ४ मिनिटांत एक अंश पुढे जाते आणि दर सुमारे २ तासांनी पूर्ण राशी बदलते. म्हणजेच तुम्ही जर रात्री ८:४० च्या आसपास जन्मला असता तर लग्न मिथुन असू शकले असते, ९:४३ ला कर्क लग्न (२१°३९' वर) आले, आणि १०:३० पर्यंत थांबला असता तर सिंह लग्न आले असते. स्थळ — कल्याण-अंबरनाथ परिसर (अक्षांश १९°१४' उत्तर, रेखांश ७३°०६' पूर्व) — हे ठरवते की आकाश तुम्हाला कोणत्या कोनातून दिसले. टोक्यो आणि मुंबई या दोन्ही ठिकाणी एकाच क्षणी जन्मलेल्या मुलांच्या कुंडल्या पूर्णपणे वेगळ्या असतात कारण पूर्व क्षितिजावर त्या दोन्ही ठिकाणी वेगळी राशी उगवत असते. म्हणूनच जन्मस्थळ हे केवळ शहर नव्हे, अक्षांश-रेखांशापर्यंत अचूक असावे लागते.

Learning Note — Birth-time rectification: why precision matters / शिकण्याची नोंद — जन्मवेळ-शुद्धीकरण: अचूकता का

Rule: The Lagna moves through 360° in 24 hours — that is 1° every 4 minutes. So a 4-minute error in birth time shifts the Lagna by 1°. A 30-minute error shifts it 7-8°, often enough to push the Lagna into the previous or next nakshatra-pada, which changes the Navamsa Lagna entirely. A 2-hour error usually moves the Lagna into a different sign, which rewrites the entire chart from scratch. Time is the single most fragile input. Date is forgiving; place is forgiving (within a city); time is not.

Applied to this chart: Pushkar's recorded birth time is 21:43 on 17 December 2003. At this moment the Lagna sits at 21°39′53″ Cancer — already deep into Aslesha nakshatra (Pada 2). If the actual birth time were 21:37 (six minutes earlier), the Lagna would be ~20°10′ Cancer — still Aslesha but on Pada 1; the D-9 Navamsa Lagna would shift from Scorpio to Libra and the whole marriage reading changes flavour. If birth were 21:49 (six minutes later), Lagna would be ~23°10′ Cancer — still Aslesha Pada 3, but inching closer to the Cancer-Leo sign cusp that arrives around 22:05. Within plus-or-minus ten minutes the chart is essentially stable; beyond that the Aslesha-Magha boundary becomes a real risk.

How to rectify a birth time you are uncertain about: (1) Collect 3-5 verifiable life events with known dates — school graduation, first job, first relationship, parent's milestone, foreign travel, major illness. Check which Vimshottari sub-period (AD or PD) activated when. The rectified time is the one whose dasha-windows match the actual events. (2) Ask the mother or hospital records — Indian birth certificates often round to the nearest 5-15 minutes, so the recorded value may be ±5 minutes. (3) Cross-check via a Lagna-rising calculator — if uncertain by ±5 minutes, the Lagna degree is usually still correct; if uncertain by ±15 minutes, run two charts and see which reads truer against past events.

Alternative readings: If birth time is uncertain by more than ±30 minutes, do not trust the chart for fine timing — use only the Mahadasha and Antardasha readings, where larger time-windows tolerate minor Lagna shifts. If uncertain by more than ±2 hours, the entire Lagna is suspect — start over with rectification, do not predict from the chart until the time is anchored. A serious consultant always asks the client: "how confident are you in your birth time, on a scale of 1-10?" The answer determines the depth at which they can read.

2.3 What is Panchanga?पंचांग म्हणजे काय?

Panchanga means "five limbs" — pancha (five) + anga (limb). It is the traditional Hindu calendar system, but more than a calendar — it is a description of the spiritual quality of a moment. Five elements together describe what kind of day, hour, and minute you were born into.

पंचांग या शब्दात दोन भाग आहेत — पंच म्हणजे पाच, आणि अंग म्हणजे शरीराचे भाग. म्हणजे "पाच अंगांचा एक तपशील." पंचांग हा पारंपरिक हिंदू कॅलेंडरचा भाग असला तरी तो फक्त "आज कोणती तारीख, कोणता दिवस" एवढेच सांगत नाही — तो जन्माच्या क्षणाची आध्यात्मिक गुणवत्ता वर्णन करतो. कोणत्याही क्षणाला आकाशात पाच गोष्टी एकत्रच चालू असतात: कोणती तिथी चालू आहे (चंद्र-दिवस), कोणता वार आहे (आठवड्याचा दिवस), चंद्र कोणत्या नक्षत्रात आहे, सूर्य-चंद्र-रेखांशाच्या बेरजेवरून कोणता योग बनतो, आणि कोणते करण चालू आहे. ही पाच अंगे एकत्र मिळून त्या क्षणाची "स्वभाव-गुणवत्ता" तयार होते — उदा. एखादा क्षण उत्साही असेल, एखादा शांत, एखादा तीव्र, एखादा प्रवासाला योग्य, एखादा निर्णयाला अयोग्य. म्हणजेच पंचांग म्हणजे "हवामान-अहवाल" आहे — फक्त वेळेच्या नव्हे, तर त्या वेळेच्या आध्यात्मिक हवामानाचा.

1. Tithi · तिथी

The lunar day. Based on the angular distance between Sun and Moon. There are 30 tithis in a lunar month — 15 of the waxing fortnight (Shukla Paksha) and 15 of the waning fortnight (Krishna Paksha).

चंद्र-दिवस. सूर्य-चंद्रातील कोनीय अंतरावर आधारित. एका चांद्रमासात ३० तिथी.

2. Vara · वार

The weekday. Each of the seven days is ruled by a planet: Sunday-Sun, Monday-Moon, Tuesday-Mars, Wednesday-Mercury, Thursday-Jupiter, Friday-Venus, Saturday-Saturn.

आठवड्याचा वार. प्रत्येक वाराचा एक ग्रह स्वामी.

3. Nakshatra · नक्षत्र

The lunar mansion the Moon occupies at birth. There are 27 nakshatras, each a slice of about 13°20′ along the zodiac.

जन्मवेळी चंद्र असलेले नक्षत्र. एकूण २७.

4. Yoga · योग

(Different from "yoga" meaning planetary combination.) A specific calculation based on the sum of Sun and Moon longitudes. There are 27 yogas, with names like Vishkambha, Priti, Ayushman, Saubhagya, Shobhana — each carrying a quality.

सूर्य-चंद्र रेखांशांच्या बेरजेवर आधारित. २७ योग.

5. Karana · करण

Half a tithi. Each tithi is split into two karanas. There are 11 karanas in total — 7 movable, 4 fixed. Used in muhurta (electional astrology) more than natal.

अर्ध तिथी. ११ करण.

Beyond the five limbs, classical Panchangas note Rahukala, Yamaganda, Gulika, Hora, Choghadiya — minor sub-divisions of the day used for muhurta. We will not go into all; the five limbs are enough for our purpose.

पाच अंगांशिवाय राहूकाळ, यमगंड, गुलिक, होरा, चौघडिया हे उपविभाग असतात — मुख्यतः मुहूर्तासाठी.

Learning Note — Read any panchanga in 60 seconds: a 5-question shortcut / शिकण्याची नोंद — कोणताही पंचांग ६० सेकंदांत वाचा

Rule: A panchanga's five limbs combine to describe the energetic quality of a moment. For natal interpretation, asking five quick questions in sequence yields a usable portrait without deep classical study.

The five questions:

  1. Tithi → what does the Moon-Sun relationship say about appetite? Waxing Moon (Shukla Paksha) = outward energy, performative, growing. Waning Moon (Krishna Paksha) = inward energy, reflective, simplifying. Specific tithi number (1-15) refines further — Navami = devotional courage, Ekadashi = renunciation-flavour, Ashtami = sharp choice, Purnima/Amavasya = peak intensity.
  2. Vara → what is the day-lord's energy? Sunday (Sun) = authority/father, Monday (Moon) = mother/intuition, Tuesday (Mars) = action/courage, Wednesday (Mercury) = intellect/communication, Thursday (Jupiter) = wisdom/optimism, Friday (Venus) = love/beauty, Saturday (Saturn) = discipline/restriction. The day-lord pulses through the native's life forever.
  3. Nakshatra → what is the deeper psychological pattern? Each of the 27 has a deity, a symbol, and a character — Hasta (hand, Savitur, dexterity), Rohini (cart, Brahma, creation), Pushya (udder, Brihaspati, nourishment). Pada (1-4) refines to a Navamsa sign, which sharpens the marriage and dharma reading.
  4. Yoga (nitya) → what intensity does the moment carry? Auspicious yogas (Shobhana, Sukarma, Saubhagya/Sobhagya) bring ease; intense yogas (Vyatipata, Vaidhriti, Vishkambha) bring depth-through-difficulty. There are 27 nitya yogas, mapped to a moving Sun+Moon longitude sum. Do not confuse with placement-yogas like Harsha or Raja-yoga; nitya yogas are time-quality, not chart-combinations.
  5. Karana → fixed or movable life-base? Movable karana (Bava, Balava, Kaulava, Taitila, Garaja, Vanija, Vishti) = a life of motion, travel, change. Fixed karana (Shakuni, Chatushpada, Naga, Kimstughna) = a life of rootedness, settled-place, hereditary work.

Applied to this chart: (1) Shukla Navami → outward, devotional, courage-flavoured appetite; "do the brave good thing" energy. (2) Wednesday Mercury vara → intellect-default temperament; problem appears → response is to analyse, communicate, find the pattern. (3) Hasta Pada 1 → hand-skilled, dexterous, Aries-pada-of-initiation; the hand that does precise work and that initiates. (4) Sobhagya yoga → auspicious yoga; ease in fortune, grace in transitions, things tend to "work out." (5) Taitila karana (movable) → life of motion and mobility; relocations, transit-rich environment, multiple-domain career expected. Composite portrait, in one sentence: "a courageous-but-analytical, hand-skilled, grace-favoured, mobility-prone soul" — and the panchanga said all of this before we looked at a single planet's house position.

Alternative reading (when limbs conflict): Sometimes the panchanga gives contradictory signals — e.g., Shukla Paksha (outward) but Mercury vara (analytical). When this happens, the underlying personality is paradoxical: an extrovert who thinks before speaking, a confident person who internally double-checks. The conflict is the gift; it produces unusual depth-plus-execution combinations. Watch for this pattern in any chart with mixed-energy panchanga — typically these are the highest-impact lives because they integrate opposites that most people experience as either/or.

या कुंडलीला लागू: (१) शुक्ल नवमी → बाहेरून, भक्ती-धैर्य; "धाडसाने योग्य गोष्ट कर" ऊर्जा. (२) बुधवार बुध वार → बुद्धी-प्राथमिक स्वभाव; समस्या समोर येताच विश्लेषण, संवाद, नमुना-शोध. 2.5 Reading Panchanga as a Personपंचांगातून व्यक्तिमत्त्व

Even before we look at a single planet's house position, the Panchanga has already sketched a portrait. Let us trust the sketch, because every detail will be confirmed and amplified later in the book.

A Navami-born person carries the energy of "devotional courage" — the willingness to do the difficult-but-right thing because it is right, not because it is easy. The tithi of Rama-navami and Durga-navami is a tithi of dharmic action: holding the line, defending the principle, finishing the job. Navami natives often have an inner sense of "what should be done," and they suffer more from compromise than from struggle.

नवमी जन्म: नवमीला जन्मलेल्या व्यक्तीत "भक्ती-धैर्य" असते — कठीण-पण-योग्य गोष्ट योग्य आहे म्हणून करण्याची तयारी, कारण ती सोपी आहे म्हणून नव्हे. रामनवमी आणि दुर्गानवमीची ही तिथी धर्म-कृतीची तिथी आहे: मर्यादा सांभाळणे, तत्त्व पाळणे, काम पूर्ण करणे. नवमी-जन्मी लोकांना "काय करायला हवे" याचे आंतरिक भान असते, आणि तडजोडीत त्यांना संघर्षापेक्षा जास्त त्रास होतो. वडिलधाऱ्यांचे शब्द, परंपरा, आणि "बरोबर मार्ग" यांचा अंतर्मनात आदर असतो — हा आदर बाहेर नेहमी प्रकट होत नाही, पण निर्णयाच्या क्षणी तो वर येतो आणि व्यक्तीला सोप्या पर्यायाऐवजी कठीण-योग्य पर्याय निवडायला लावतो.

A Wednesday-born person carries Mercury in the bloodstream. The default response to a problem is to understand it — analyse, name, model, then act. Speech tends to be quick, precise, and information-rich; humour is often verbal and pattern-based; learning happens through reading, conversation, and "figuring things out." There is a built-in love for systems, languages, and well-designed tools. Wednesday-natives are wired for the modern world's information work — code, finance, research, writing, anything where the medium is symbol.

बुधवार जन्म: बुधवारी जन्मलेल्या व्यक्तीच्या रक्तातच बुध असतो. कोणतीही अडचण आली की पहिली प्रतिक्रिया "समजून घे" — विश्लेषण कर, नाव दे, नमुना ओळख, मग कृती कर. बोलणे झटपट, अचूक, माहितीने भरलेले; विनोद बहुधा शाब्दिक आणि नमुना-आधारित; शिकणे वाचन, संवाद, आणि "हे कसे चालते" शोधण्यातून. प्रणाली, भाषा, चांगली बनवलेली साधने यांच्याकडे जन्मजात ओढ. आधुनिक जगाच्या माहिती-कामासाठी — कोड, फायनान्स, संशोधन, लेखन, जिथे माध्यम चिन्ह आहे अशा कोणत्याही क्षेत्रासाठी — बुधवार-जातक नैसर्गिकरीत्या तयार असतो. कन्या ही पुष्करची चंद्र-राशी सुद्धा बुधाचीच, म्हणून ही बुध-छटा दुहेरी मजबूत.

A Hasta-born person is hand-skilled. The very name hasta means "hand," and the nakshatra's symbol is an open palm — the hand that crafts, the hand that heals, the hand that grasps the exact small object. Hasta natives are drawn to work that uses fine motor skill and precision: writing, drawing, instrument-playing, surgery, repair, micro-craft, code (the keyboard is the modern hasta-instrument). The deity, Savitur, is the radiant-craftsman aspect of the Sun — divine maker. Pada 1, falling in Aries navamsa, sharpens the hand with initiative — Pushkar's hand will not wait passively; it will start the work.

हस्त जन्म: हस्त नक्षत्राचा अर्थच "हात." चिन्ह — उघडी पंजा. हस्त-जन्मी लोक हाताने सूक्ष्म व अचूक काम करणारे असतात: लेखन, चित्र, वाद्य, शस्त्रक्रिया, दुरुस्ती, सूक्ष्म-कला, कोड (आधुनिक हस्त-साधन म्हणजे कीबोर्ड). देवता सवितृ — सूर्याचे तेजस्वी-कारागीर रूप, दैवी निर्माता. पाद १ मेष-नवमांशात पडतो, त्यामुळे हाताला पुढाकाराची धार — पुष्करचा हात निष्क्रियपणे थांबणार नाही; तो काम सुरू करेल. नक्षत्र-स्वामी चंद्र, आणि चंद्र स्वतः हस्ताच्याच पादात — म्हणजे चंद्र-शुद्ध संरचना. भावना, मन, आणि हस्त-कौशल्य एकाच धाग्यात गुंफलेले.

A Sobhagya yoga birth is an ancient Indian way of saying: "fortune will tend to favour this person." It is not a guarantee of an easy life, but it is a soft tilt toward grace — strangers offer help at the right moments, opportunities turn up at the right doors, mistakes get forgiven more often than they should. The yoga's name is literally good-fortune (sau-bhagya), and the tradition counts it among the most benevolent of the 27 nitya yogas.

सौभाग्य योग: सौभाग्य या नावाचा अर्थच "चांगले भाग्य." हे जीवन सोपे होईल याची हमी नाही, पण कृपेकडे एक हलकासा कल — योग्य क्षणी अनोळखी मदत करतात, संधी योग्य दारात येतात, चुका हव्या त्यापेक्षा अधिक वेळा माफ होतात. नित्य-योगांच्या २७ पैकी सौभाग्य हा सर्वात उदार आणि शुभ मानला जातो. कुठेतरी अडकल्यास "वाट सापडेल" ही आंतरिक खात्री हे या योगाचे वैशिष्ट्य. ही श्रद्धा भोळी नसून, पुनःपुन्हा अनुभवातून मिळवलेली असते — आणि हाच विश्वास पुढच्या संधीला खुलेपणाने सामोरे जाण्याची हिंमत देतो.

A Taitila karana birth signals movable life-base — motion is the default, not the exception. Taitila natives change residences, change roles, travel across geographies and disciplines; their lives have many chapters rather than one long settled one. The karana also carries a sweetness-of-temperament note in classical descriptions — Taitila is one of the more agreeable of the seven chara karanas.

तैतिल करण: तैतिल — सात चल करणांपैकी एक. म्हणजे जीवनात गती हाच डिफॉल्ट, स्थिरता अपवाद. तैतिल-जन्मी लोक निवासस्थान बदलतात, भूमिका बदलतात, अनेक भौगोलिक प्रदेशांत व विषयांत प्रवास करतात; आयुष्यात अनेक अध्याय असतात, एकच लांब स्थिर अध्याय नव्हे. शास्त्रीय वर्णनात तैतिलाला "मधुर स्वभाव" अशी जोड दिलेली — सात चल करणांत हे अधिक सुसंवादी म्हणून ओळखले जाते. विरोधाभास नाही — गती असली तरी ती कडवटपणा न आणता घडते.

A Shukla Paksha birth produces an outward turn. The waxing Moon symbolizes growth — moonlight is increasing, which trains the eyes (and the personality) to expand into more light. Shukla-paksha natives often feel best when something is being built — a project, a skill, a relationship, a body of work. They are less suited to seasons of pure withdrawal; they need a visible direction of growth.

शुक्ल पक्ष जन्म: चंद्र दर पंधरवड्याला वाढतो आणि घटतो. शुक्ल पक्ष म्हणजे वाढता चंद्र. या काळात जन्म घेणाऱ्यांचा स्वभाव बहिर्मुख, विस्तार-प्रवण असतो. वाढत्या चांदण्यात डोळे (आणि व्यक्तिमत्त्व) अधिक प्रकाशात विस्तारायला शिकतात. शुक्ल-जन्मी माणसांना काहीतरी घडवण्यात समाधान वाटते — प्रकल्प, कौशल्य, नातेसंबंध, कामाचे शरीर. केवळ माघार घेण्याच्या ऋतूत त्यांना अस्वस्थ वाटते; त्यांना वाढीची एक दृश्य दिशा हवी असते — "मी कुठेतरी जातो आहे" ही जाणीव.

The grid is the practical Marathi-Hindu interface for kundali-milan, sanskara timing, and householder muhurta. Pushkar's avakahada cells are recorded below.

When a marriage proposal arrives, the family does not first read the natal chart — they first read the avakahada. Within ninety seconds the priest can check: gana compatibility (Deva-Deva pair = excellent), yoni compatibility (Mahish-Cow is friendly; Mahish-Horse is mismatched), nadi compatibility (different nadis = no dosha; same nadi = caveat), varna ordering (boy's varna ≥ girl's varna preferred in tradition), vashya domain (Manav-Manav fine; Manav-Vanchar problematic). Avakahada also fixes the auspicious naming-letter (Pushkar's Poo- syllable from Hasta Pada 1), which in turn anchors the legal name, the deity-mantra-letter, and the engraved name on family records. In short: the avakahada is the practical entry-point to Vedic-Hindu life decisions; the natal chart is the deeper study that follows.

2.7 The Birth Mahadashaजन्म महादशा

Vimshottari Dasha — the 120-year wheel — does not start fresh at birth. It depends on which nakshatra the Moon was in. Each nakshatra is "owned" by one of the nine grahas (a 27-nakshatra cycle of 9 planets, repeating three times). The Mahadasha at birth begins with the lord of the nakshatra the Moon was in at that exact moment — but typically the child enters mid-stream, with only a partial balance of that period remaining.

Learning Note — How Vimshottari birth-balance is computed / शिकण्याची नोंद — विमशोत्तरी जन्म-शिल्लक कशी मोजली जाते

Rule: The 120-year Vimshottari cycle is anchored to the Moon's exact position at birth, specifically to the nakshatra-fraction the Moon has already traversed. The Mahadasha at birth belongs to the nakshatra-lord of the natal Moon's nakshatra. The balance of that MD at birth = (full MD years) × (fraction of nakshatra remaining). Two ingredients are needed: the nakshatra-lord (which gives the planet whose MD runs first), and the Moon's nakshatra-percentage at birth (which gives how much of that MD is still unconsumed).

The math, step by step (for this chart): Moon at birth was at 13°10′12″ Virgo (sidereal, Lahiri ayanamsa 23°54′31″). Hasta spans 10°00′ Virgo to 23°20′ Virgo (a 13°20′ window). The Moon at 13°10′ Virgo has traversed: 13°10′ − 10°00′ = 3°10′ of the nakshatra. Remaining = 13°20′ − 3°10′ = 10°10′. Fraction remaining = 10°10′ ÷ 13°20′ = 610 ÷ 800 = 0.7625. Moon's full Mahadasha = 10 years. Balance at birth = 10 × 0.7625 = 7.625 years = 7 years, 7 months, 15 days (the panchanga's classical print shows "7Y 7M 14D"; the one-day variance comes from rounding the ayanamsa to the nearest arc-second). Moon MD therefore ends on 2 August 2011; Mars MD begins immediately after, running 7 years to 2 August 2018; Rahu MD begins thereafter, running 18 years to 2 August 2036.

Why this matters: The Mahadasha at birth shapes the first chapter of life. Native born with a long balance of a difficult dasha (say Saturn MD with 18 years left) carries that planet's flavour through childhood and adolescence. Pushkar's case is the opposite: born with nearly the full 7.6-year balance of the gentlest dasha — Moon — which means the entire first chapter of life (from infancy almost to age 8) was bathed in Moon's mother-emotional-intuitive flavour. This is a strikingly soft, well-mothered start; Moon-MD childhood tends to produce emotionally rich, language-early, mother-bonded children.

Applied to this chart: 7y 7m of Moon MD at birth = Moon's signature is the foundational dasha-flavour of infancy, toddlerhood, primary-school years; gentleness, emotional attunement, mother-centred world. From August 2011 to August 2018, Mars MD ran (ages 7.5 to 14.5) — middle-school and early-teens, with Mars-flavoured ambition, sport-or-physical-skill emphasis, and the first sharp identity assertions of pre-adolescence. From August 2018 onwards, Rahu MD opens (ages 14.5 to 32.5) — the current 18-year chapter of identity-experimentation, foreign or unconventional exposure, technology and ambition, and the building-out of an unconventional adult life.

Alternative readings: If the Moon were at Hasta Pada 4 (closer to 23° Virgo) instead of Pada 1, the balance would be much smaller — only ~2 years of Moon MD remaining. Mars MD would have started around age 2 and Rahu around age 9, compressing the gentle-Moon phase and accelerating the more assertive dashas.
